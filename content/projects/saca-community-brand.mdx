## Overview

Beyond the marquee events, a student org lives or dies on its weekly cadence — meetings,
office hours, elections, recaps. This is the connective tissue: graphics that kept the
South Asian Cultural Association legible and present in the feed all year, plus the
physical touchpoints (merch, the office door tag) that carried the brand off-screen.
SACA operates out of UO's Multicultural Center — the same community the
[MCC Scheduler](/work/mcc-scheduler) was built for.

The 2024–25 drop, anchored on a member-designed lotus mark (design-contest winner),
