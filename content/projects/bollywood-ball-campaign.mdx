## Overview

Bollywood Ball is the South Asian Cultural Association's flagship formal at the University
of Oregon. The 2025 campaign ran as a coherent arc on Instagram: a cinematic announcement,
a countdown series to sustain hype, a style guide that made the event approachable for
first-time attendees, and utility posts that answered every logistics question before it
was asked.

## The announcement

A vintage-cinema treatment — a film-reel story frame and a Bollywood-poster teaser — set
the tone for the night.
