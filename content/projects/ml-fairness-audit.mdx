## Overview

A toolkit for measuring subgroup disparities in classification models, paired with a
short empirical study across several public datasets. The reporting layer renders
the findings as accessible, screen-reader-friendly dashboards.

It lives in the `research` cluster but shares `accessibility` and `data-viz` with the
design system — a deliberate `design`↔`research` bridge built from both a `related`
link and shared tags.
