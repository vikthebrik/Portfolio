## Overview

A feature-extraction and tracking pipeline designed to run inside a tight control
loop. Frames move through detection, descriptor extraction, and tracking with no
intermediate copies, sustaining camera framerate on a constrained SoC.

It feeds the SLAM backend and runs on the mapping drone, so it sits between the
`tech` and `research` clusters — exactly the cross-pillar bridge the graph is
meant to surface.
