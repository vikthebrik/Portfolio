## Overview

The South Asian Cultural Association's calendar runs on its cultural anchors — Holi,
Diwali, Utsav. Each event got its own visual identity: saturated color-throw photography
for Holi, an engraved-illustration deity motif on deep maroon for Diwali, hand-drawn
warmth for the community nights. The constraint was consistency *within* an event and
contrast *between* them, so each one felt like its own moment in the feed.
